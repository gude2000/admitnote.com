// Features

Everything a hospitalist needs.

Built by a physician who got tired of spending more time documenting than doctoring.

🎙️
AI-Powered Transcription
Powered by Whisper and Claude. Understands clinical language, drug names, and medical shorthand. 120+ custom pronunciations, 450+ medical terms.
📋
Complete H&P Generation
CC, HPI, ROS (13 systems), PMH, PSH, Medications, Allergies, Family/Social History, Physical Exam (9 systems), Labs, Imaging — all auto-populated.
⚕️
Evidence-Based A&P
Assessment, Plan, Differential Diagnosis, ICD-10 codes, and CPT admission code — referencing ACC/AHA, IDSA, GOLD, KDIGO, ADA, and Surviving Sepsis guidelines.
🔒
HIPAA-Oriented Design
Audio deleted after transcription. Notes auto-delete in 48h. All data on-device only. No cloud sync. No analytics. No ads. Encrypted HTTPS transmission.
💡
Smart Dictation
Hold-to-dictate or hands-free recording. Voice commands: "scratch that," "next line," "go to end." Live transcript preview while recording.
📊
Natural Language Shortcuts
"PE normal except..." generates a full exam. "ROS negative except..." populates all 13 systems. "Labs normal except..." expands the full panel.

// Privacy & HIPAA

Your patients' data
stays on your device.

AdmitNote is designed from the ground up to minimize data collection and protect patient privacy.

✓
Audio deleted immediately
Recordings are permanently deleted from your device the moment transcription completes. Never retained, never uploaded.
✓
On-device storage only
All notes stored locally using iOS encrypted storage. No cloud sync, no external database, no server-side PHI.
✓
48-hour auto-deletion
Unlocked notes automatically delete after 48 hours, minimizing PHI retention. Lock notes to keep them longer.
✓
No tracking. No ads. Ever.
Zero analytics SDKs, no advertising identifiers, no crash reporting with PHI, no third-party data sharing of any kind.
✓
Encrypted transmission
Clinical text sent to the AI API via HTTPS/TLS only, handled under a Business Associate Agreement with AWS.
✓
No account required
No login, no account creation, no personal information collected. The app opens directly to your notes list.
