// First Launch

Privacy Notice

On first launch, AdmitNote shows a full privacy disclosure before any clinical data is transmitted. This explains exactly what is sent, who receives it, and how your data is protected.

🔵 What data is sent
Clinical text sent to Anthropic Claude API when you tap "Restructure Full Note" or "Transcribe." Never your name, device ID, or personal information.
🏢 Who receives it
Anthropic (Claude API) via encrypted HTTPS. Anthropic does not use your clinical text to train AI models per our agreement.
✅ How data is protected
Audio deleted immediately · On-device storage only · No PHI on external servers · Notes auto-delete in 48h · BAA with AWS
Shown once Cannot be swiped away Stored in UserDefaults

// Note View

Structured H&P Note

After transcription, your note appears fully structured with all 14 H&P sections populated. The AI routes every piece of clinical information to the correct field automatically.

Chief Complaint HPI ROS × 13 PMH PSH Medications Allergies Family Hx Social Hx Vitals PE × 9 Labs Imaging Assessment & Plan
🟠 Add More Info
The orange button at the top lets you dictate additional findings at any time — labs returning, PE findings, new meds. The AI routes everything to the right section.
🔵 Restructure Full Note
The blue button regenerates the entire note — re-sorts all sections, regenerates Assessment & Plan, Differential, ICD-10 codes, and CPT code.
✏️ Inline Editing
Tap any section header or the pencil icon to open a full-screen editor for that field. Autosaves 3 seconds after your last edit.
// Dictation

Add More Info

The most powerful feature in AdmitNote. Dictate any clinical information in any order — the AI parses everything and routes each piece to the correct H&P section.

  • 1
    Hold to Dictate — press and hold, speak, release. Best for short additions.
  • 2
    Record / Stop — tap to start hands-free recording, tap again to stop.
  • 3
    Tap Done — returns to note view with content ready to restructure.
  • 4
    Tap Restructure — AI incorporates everything and regenerates all sections.
Natural Language Shortcuts
🗣️ "PE normal except crackles at bases"
Generates a complete 9-system Physical Exam with all systems documented as normal except the lungs.
🗣️ "ROS negative except chest pain and SOB"
Populates all 13 Review of Systems as Non-contributory except the specified positives.
🗣️ "PMH diabetes, HTN, CKD stage 3"
Routes each item directly to Past Medical History as a formatted bullet list.

// Inline Editing

Vitals & Field Editing

Every field in the note is individually editable. The Vitals section has dedicated fields for each parameter with individual microphone buttons for direct dictation into each field.

🎤 Per-Field Dictation
Each vital sign field has its own microphone button. Tap it and speak — the value is transcribed directly into that field. The red "Listening..." indicator confirms active dictation.
⬇️ Next Field Button
The "Next Field (2)" button at the bottom jumps to the next empty section. The number in parentheses shows how many fields still need content.
💾 Autosave
Tap "Save Data" to save immediately, or let autosave handle it — changes are saved automatically 3 seconds after your last edit.
